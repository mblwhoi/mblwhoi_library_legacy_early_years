Marine Biological Laboratory "The Early Years"


Alfred Francis Huettner (1882 - 1955)
William Peter Proctor towing skiff along shore of Great Harbor, 1923

William Proctor (1872 - 1951) was a man of many talents - Zoologist, industrialist and heir to the Proctor & Gamble fortune. This atmospheric photograph was taken by his colleague and friend, Alfred Huettner, on a balmy summer day in Woods Hole. Huettner subsequently dedicated his classic embryology textbook to proctor, the "Naturalist and Scholar"
